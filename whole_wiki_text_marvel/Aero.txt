

Aero (Lei Ling) is a fictional Chinese superhero appearing in the Marvel Universe. The character was created for the Chinese market by artist Keng and writer Zhou Liefen in a collaboration between Marvel Comics and NetEase.After debuting in Chinese digital comics, Aero made her U.S. comics debut in War of the Realms: New Agents of Atlas before starring in her own series. Her series features translations of the original Chinese comics and new material by Greg Pak teaming up with Wave.Lei Ling is an architect based in Shanghai, China. As Aero, she has the ability to harness the power of the air.
